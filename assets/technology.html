How it works

  • 1We gather 15-25 pictures of each location or object and upload them to PARWorks.
  • 2The pictures are compiled into a three-dimensional point cloud of the object.
  • 3Using a few of the original image, we mark up the model and create overlays.
  • 4You take an image of a site with your mobile phone. Using advanced image recognition techniques, we match your image against the available 3D models, and return information about the overlays in your particular image.
